About Company

Blitz or Blind Matez is a smart cane designed specifically to help people with visual impairments identify and interact with their surrounding environment. A smart cane is a cane used to help improve the orientation and mobility of people with visual impairments. This device is equipped with GPS that can determine the user’s last known location, helping family members locate them if they get lost.

When the smart cane detects an object, the system will process the captured video and perform object detection using a CNN. If an object is detected, the smart cane will notify the user through Bluetooth earphones. When a visually impaired user feels lost and presses the emergency button on the smart cane, the cane will send the user’s last known location.

Vision

To empower blind and visually impaired people to navigate safely and confidently, enabling greater independence through intelligent assistive technology.

Mission

Our mission is to:

  • Enhance orientation and mobility for blind and visually impaired users through a smart cane that supports safer daily navigation.
  • Provide real-time obstacle awareness by detecting objects from captured video using CNN-based object detection and delivering audio notifications via Bluetooth earphones.
  • Improve user safety in emergencies by enabling an emergency button feature that helps share the user’s last known location when they feel lost.
  • Deliver an accessible, reliable, and user-friendly experience that fits naturally into everyday use.
  • Act Responsibly: Operate with integrity and environmental consciousness
